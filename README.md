# LI - Logística Integrada

LiApp is an integrated logistics platform designed to connect people and businesses with professionals capable of performing logistics services efficiently and reliably.

Through a digital ecosystem, LiApp simplifies the process of requesting and executing services such as deliveries, freight transport, moving assistance, equipment rental, and storage solutions. The platform focuses on providing practical, agile, and transparent logistics services for individuals and companies.

## About the Company

LiApp was created with the goal of simplifying logistics and expanding access to delivery and transportation services. By connecting demand with available service providers, the platform enables faster operations, improved service management, and new opportunities for professionals in the logistics sector.

The company operates through a technology-driven platform that integrates mobile applications, backend services, and operational tools to support logistics operations.

## Mission

Provide opportunities and solutions that simplify logistics and connect people through efficient delivery and transportation services.

## Vision

To become a national reference in integrated logistics services through technology and innovation.
